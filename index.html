About Just In Case

The JIC project delivers a self-contained LLM-powered conversational search engine over curated emergency survival PDFs that works without the Internet.

What's Included

  • Survival guides and emergency procedures
  • Medical references and first aid
  • Agricultural and engineering resources
  • Educational materials for long-term resilience

How It Works

This system uses local AI models to search through indexed PDF documents, providing instant answers to survival questions even when offline.

Quick Tips

  • Ask specific questions for best results
  • Click on document links to view full PDFs
  • Keep this system updated with relevant local information
  • Use our Preparedness Coach to assess your readiness
